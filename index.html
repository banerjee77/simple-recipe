Mixed Fried Rice

Mixed Fried Rice is one of the most popular dish across Asia; mostly East Asia, South East Asia and SOuth Asia. It is a mixture of a slightly fried rice, chicken, prawns and veggies!! It is easy to make as well as extremely tasty. And guess what, they're healthy too!!!

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Chicken
  • Prawns
  • Veggies

Instructions

  1. Fry the rice in little olive oil.
  2. Add some veggies like carrots, beans etc to the fried rice
  3. Boil and cook the chicken in very less oil
  4. Repeat the same process with the prawns
  5. You can add some scrambled eggs too!
  6. Take the chicken, prawns and scrambled eggs to the fried rice; THAT'S IT!
